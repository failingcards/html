      <section class="main-section" id="minecraft">

        <header>Minecraft</header>

        <p>Minecraft is a sandbox survival game developed by Mojang Studios. It allows players to explore, build, and survive in a block-based world made up of infinite terrain and various biomes.</p>

        <p>Players can play in Creative mode to build freely or Survival mode to gather resources and battle mobs. Multiplayer and modding are key parts of the Minecraft experience.</p>

        <code>// Create a crafting table in Survival:

Open crafting menu -> place 4 wooden planks in 2x2 grid</code>

        <ul>

          <li>Released: November 18, 2011</li>

          <li>Platforms: PC, Console, Mobile</li>

          <li>Genre: Sandbox, Survival, Adventure</li>

        </ul>

      </section>

        <header>NBA 2K</header>

        <p>NBA 2K is a basketball simulation video game series developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. It offers a realistic basketball experience with various game modes.</p>

        <p>The game includes MyCareer, where players create a custom athlete, MyTeam for card-based team building, and online matches for competitive play.</p>

          <li>Platforms: PC, PlayStation, Xbox</li>

          <li>Genre: Sports Simulation</li>

        </ul>

        <p>Players gather resources, build structures, and use a wide range of weapons. Fortnite is known for its frequent updates, crossovers, and seasonal events.</p>
