GTA V

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar Games. It features a massive map with three main protagonists and countless side missions.

The game combines driving, shooting, heists, and exploration, and includes an online mode known as GTA Online, where players can interact in a shared world.

  • Genre: Action-adventure, Open world
Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox survival game developed by Mojang Studios. It allows players to explore, build, and survive in a block-based world made up of infinite terrain and various biomes.

Players can play in Creative mode to build freely or Survival mode to gather resources and battle mobs. Multiplayer and modding are key parts of the Minecraft experience.

  • Released: November 18, 2011
  • Platforms: PC, Console, Mobile
  • Genre: Sandbox, Survival, Adventure
NBA 2K

NBA 2K is a basketball simulation video game series developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. It offers a realistic basketball experience with various game modes.

The game includes MyCareer, where players create a custom athlete, MyTeam for card-based team building, and online matches for competitive play.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a battle royale game developed by Epic Games. It features fast-paced gameplay where 100 players fight to be the last one standing.

Players gather resources, build structures, and use a wide range of weapons. Fortnite is known for its frequent updates, crossovers, and seasonal events.

  • Released: July 25, 2017
  • Platforms: PC, Console, Mobile
  • Genre: Battle Royale, Survival
Roblox

Roblox is an online platform and game creation system that allows users to program and play games created by other users. It supports user-generated content across millions of games.

Games on Roblox are built using Lua scripting and range from obstacle courses to simulators, role-playing games, and shooters. It is extremely popular among younger audiences.
